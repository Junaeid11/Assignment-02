BMI calculator

BMI, or Body Mass Index, is a simple calculation used to assess whether a person has a healthy body weight for their height.. It’s commonly used to categorize individuals into different weight status categories, such as underweight, normal weight, overweight, and obese.

BMI calculator Chart

BMI Weight Status
Bellow 18.5 Underweight
18.5-24.9 Healthy
25.0-29.9 Overweight
30.0-and Above Obese
